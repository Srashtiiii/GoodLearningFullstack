Comic-Con!

A comic con, or comic book convention, is a fan convention that focuses on comic books and related pop culture. Comic cons are often multi-day events held at convention centers, hotels, or college campuses. Comic cons can also include other forms of pop culture entertainment, such as movies, TV shows, and video games.
